FAQ

Answers about temporary QR codes and expiring links.

Why would I want expiration?

Because not everything needs to live online forever — especially previews, work-in-progress files, personal info, or temporary event data.

Do you store history?

No. TempQR does not provide an account/dashboard or a history list. Once a link expires, it’s gone.

Do you require an account?

No login, no signup, no dashboard.

Can I use it for client work?

Yes — especially useful for temporary previews and drafts (Figma/Notion, PDFs, staging links, and other review links).

Can someone recover expired links?

Expired links stop working and cannot be reactivated.
