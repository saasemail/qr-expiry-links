Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary. TempQR is designed to work without accounts and without requesting personal profile information.

Accounts
No signup required.
Tracking cookies
No intentional tracking cookies.
Purpose
Generate expiring links and QR codes.

What we process. When you generate an expiring link, the service processes the target URL and your chosen expiry duration to create the link/QR. Your browser also sends standard request data (e.g., IP address, user agent) to the hosting provider as part of normal web traffic.

How we use information. Only to provide the service, maintain security, and prevent abuse.

Third parties. The site is hosted on infrastructure providers (e.g., the hosting platform and CDN). They may process basic request logs for security and performance.

Retention. Expiring links stop working after the expiry time you select. Some technical logs may be retained by hosting providers for a limited time for security and debugging.

Contact. If you have privacy questions, use the Feedback page.

If you later add analytics/ads, update this policy accordingly.
