Temporary QR codes & expiring links (with URL masking)

TempQR lets you turn any URL into a private, temporary QR code and an expiring link. Your original URL is masked behind a TempQR link, and access stops automatically after the time you choose. This is a simple way to share links without leaving them online forever — with no tracking and no saved history.

Common ways people use TempQR

  • Private file links (Drive/Dropbox/PDF): share a link that expires after delivery.
  • Client previews (Figma/Notion/portfolio): send a temporary link that won’t stay public.
  • Events & posters: put a QR on flyers and set it to expire after the event ends.
  • Sensitive links: share personal or private content with limited-time access.

If you’re looking for a temporary QR code, an expiring QR code, or a private link that automatically expires, TempQR is built for exactly that.
