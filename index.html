Temporary QR codes & expiring links that protect your privacy

TempQR lets you turn any URL into a private, temporary QR code and an expiring link. Your original content is masked behind a TempQR link, and access stops automatically after the time you choose. This is a simple way to share your content without leaving them online forever — with no tracking and no saved history.

Common ways people use TempQR

  • Client previews (Figma/Notion/portfolio): send a private link that expires after delivery.
  • Events & posters: put a QR on flyers and set it to expire after the event ends.
  • Sensitive links: share personal or private URLs with limited-time access.
  • Short-term campaigns: promos that must stop working at a specific time.
  • Temporary redirects: mask the original URL behind a TempQR link.

If you’re looking for a temporary QR code, an expiring QR code, or a private link that automatically expires, TempQR is built for exactly that.
